CastleVania Symphony of the Night

One of the coolest pieces of cover art for a game... in reality it's the cover art from a game collection that looks vastly better than the original art.

Bloodstained Ritual of the Night

A cool game I got for $15 in 2019 and fell in love with. It's just like CastleVania but it came out 20 years later or something.

Sonic Mania

A pretty cool piece of cover art for a game, but the only way you'd ever see it was if you took it out of the box and turned it inside out.

Demon's Souls

A game that revolutionized the game industry, but it didn't sell as well as the "sequel" Dark Souls so everyone gives the credit to that one instead. What a shame, the atmosphere and artstyle of this game was fantastic, and the music was very unique and understated.

Mega Man

One of the most infamously bad pieces of game cover art. I'm surprised that this game sold at all considering people had to see it and decide "yeah I want to play this."

Pizza Tower

A game that I eagarly wait for about 5 years to play. It's very polished and incredibly well animated, it looks like a 90's cartoon.

Metal Gear Solid 3

A great game that takes place in a fake rainforest in Russia during the cold war. I have no idea what they were thinking with this one it's odd

Resident Evil Remake HD Remaster

One of the best horror games ever made, even if it is just a remake of the original playstation version. They just don't make them like this anymore.

Katamari Damacy

An odd game about rolling up everyday objects and people into a ball and launching them into space. This one has great music as well, I'd recommend Gin and Tonic as its nice and jazzy.

Super Metroid

The best videogame ever made (in my opinion). It was great music, revolutionary gameplay, a nice story told without dialogue, and cool aliens. Videogame developers have tried to replicate this game's perfect design, and none have succeeded.

Okami

This is a game I never had the chance to play, and it's a shame since the game really looks like a Japanese caligraphy painting but in 3d. Too bad I was grounded when it came out.

Super Mario Bros. 3

This is my favorite Super Mario game ever. It has a great difficulty curve and really fun levels and powerups. It even had a neat 80's cartoon, the boxart is VERY bright and yellow. It stands out garishly among a shelf of other Nintendo games.

Jaws

A movie I've never seen. I was supposed to watch it one night and fell asleep instead. It definitely has a shark in it.

The Thing (1982)

One of the best horror movies I've ever seen. It's pretty brutal so it's not for everyone.

Casablanca

An interesting movie that I watched this summer. A nice little love story with good music. I first learned about this movie because of a World History course in High School, and then I shoved it into the back of my mind until summer.

The Batman

This is my favorite (and in my opinion best) Batman film. It has great music out of all of them, great actors that play great interpretations of the characters, and it follows an intriguing storyline that I never get bored of. The movie is visually spectacular as well. I'm so excited for the sequel, but I may have to wait a couple years for it.

9

One of my favorite movies as a kid. It has really interesting character designs and some creepy robots.

Citizen Kane

Another iteresting movie that I watched this summer. Maybe not the best film of all time, but an insanely good one that holds up well.

Star Wars

A cultural revolution, this movie changed EVERYTHING. This was one of the most influential pieces of media ever created and spawned stories in all mediums, like books, music, video games, and statues/sculptures. The unfortunate thing about it is that you can't legally watch the original 1977 version, just the new tampered versions.

GoodFellas

This is a great ganster/mafia film. This is considered to be one of the best films of all time by many people, especially critics. While it is entertaining, I don't entirely agree with that statement. I enjoy Casino slightly more despite not being as iconic.

E.T.

I've never watched this movie before, I've only seen clips of it. I just really like this promotional art.

2001 a Space Odyssey

This film still holds up and deserves the praise it gets. It looks so good it is used as evidence to support moon landing conspiracy theories.

My Neighbor Totoro

One of the most iconic animated films ever made, and I watched it too late I should have watched this as a kid and not a 21 year old man.

Home Alone

A movie that I watched recently (about two weeks ago if you're reading this after November 24th) with my father and younger brother. It was a very good time, it was quite funny and had felt quite unique, even though I've seen Home Alone 2 before.

The Divine Comedy

A lot of religious stories and imagery of hell came from this book, and most of it was just made up out of nowhere.

The Catcher in the Rye

A book I read in highschool that I never quite understood the point of. I thought the main character was a bit of a loser.

Dune Messiah

The sequel to the first Dune book, I read it immediately after watching Dune part 2 this year. It's not as good but still interesting.

The Necronomicon

A collection of some of the most famous H.P Lovecraft stories, some are horror stories, others feel like weird rants from a madman.

Uzumaki

The first horror manga(Japanese comic book) I ever read. It's such a strange idea, it's about spirals.

Bridge 108

A book I read in highschool for a book report in the first month of Covid lockdowns. It has a cool dystopian world that has really simple characters. 7/10

A Thousand Splendid Suns

A novel I read in high school that left an impact on me. I'm not sure how to summarize it succinctly.

Lord of the Rings: Fellowship of the Ring

I read this book because I thought the movie was bad, I'm glad I did I LOVED the book so much more.

Batman: The Long Halloween

A cool little mystery comic, it made me realize that Batman outside of the movies is cool too.

Watchmen

One of the most well written comic books ever. The subject matter isn't for everyone however, its not a fun read.

Holes

My favorite book from childhood, I read this book nearly 10 times as a kid. The film is not as good.

The Shining

I've seem the film and didn't think it was that scary, people say the novel is better so I may give it a shot (once it's on sale).

The Great Wave of Kanagawa

This is a painting that I've seen sold everywhere, and parodied. It is so odd seeing the original version of this painting, it looks beautiful... but I've seen too many monsters drawn into the painting, the regular one looks empty to me now.

Starry Night

This is a classic painting, everyone knows this one. I've seen many people say that this is the best painting ever made, but I disagree. There were near photorealistic paintings in the 1600s, its great but not the best (in my opinion).

Impression, Sunrise

I love how the water looks in this painting, it makes me oddly thirsty. I drank some water halfway through writing my thoughts about the painting. You should too! Stay hydrated!

Night Hawks
